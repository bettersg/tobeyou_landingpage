About the Project

What if you were not born as you, but as somebody else? What if your race, gender, and other "identities" were something else? Would you still be able to go through life as you had? Would it be easier? Harder? What challenges and opportunities would you have that are different? We are building an interactive fiction game that gets to the heart of empathy, but instead of asking people to "walk a mile" in their shoes, we are asking them to "play as a character with their identity" in our game.

To Be You was conceived to help dismantle racial and religious stereotypes, and ultimately foster greater empathy and inclusiveness among Singaporeans. This idea won the top prize at the 2020 MCCY:Mission Unite Hackathon.

This is project by the non-profit volunteer-run techforgood community, better.sg. At better.sg, we brainstorm, build, and supercharge techforgood digital tools to address societal issues.
